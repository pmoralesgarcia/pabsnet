Last night I was very stoked to have attended [Young the Giant](http://www.youngthegiant.com/) live at the [Greek Theatre at UC Berkeley](https://thegreekberkeley.com/). I want to thank public transit and especially [BART](https://bart.gov) for getting me there yesterday!

The concert was such a blast! I've always wanted to see Young the Giant. I remember listening to them back in the early 2010's at my parent's house after learning about them on a blog. Blogs are cool and so is their music. 

I really enjoyed the energy all around me. People were dancing, holding hands, hugging their friends and all sorts of affection. It made me feel real happy and safe. Harmony every where. The concert made me feel emotions I haven't felt in a long time and how I reflect on my life a bit. It was nice seeing my friends have a great time as well. 

The concert was perfect. I was in heaven. 

I didn't realize that Young the Giant had released the album,  [Victory Garden](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Victory_Garden_(album)). I'm really loving the album.

This is the highlight of my week.
