This past weekend I went to San Francisco Pride! It was a beautiful sunny day in the city. The air was full of love and it was especially gay! This year was a little different. Normally I am a spectator where I cheer on all the different groups walking and showing off their cool floats. I normally take photos on film. This time I marched in parade for the first time.  It was an early day for me. I felt like like I was having my Princess Diaries moment and I also felt Britney Spears waving at people.


I had a blast hanging out with the [Grand Ducal Council of San Francisco](https://www.sfducal.org/). I got to march with Drag Queens, Dukes and Duxes and people of the court. They have been very welcoming and it's been nice interacting with the community. They are amazing people. 


I love this city. I can't image being anywhere else. Love is love!
