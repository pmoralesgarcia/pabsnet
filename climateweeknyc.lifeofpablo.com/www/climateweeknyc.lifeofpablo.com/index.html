NYC Climate Week 2023

A Wakeup Call About Climate Change

Call to Action or Official Site

Pablo Morales

Sacramento, CA

Hey, I'm Pablo! I was very fortunate to attend NYC Climate Week. It was an eye opening event and how much we have at stake to save our world. We need to take action.

There were so many events to attend. I learned so much about different technology and different projects happening through out the world. I found some very interesting and some not so interesting. Some were really about the climate and some were more corporate focused. It was so amazing to see what projects in NYC were in the works for public transit. Many know the United States lacks proper public transportation infrastructure to get away from car dependency.

I networked. with a lot of amazing people and was inspired by people and what their goals were.
